Human Nature

TouchDesigner, 2022

Human Nature was a collaborative project where I was approached by a music-technology student to create visuals for an immersive audio-visual experience.

The visuals I created were to be controlled by T-sticks, an interactive instrument that was covered in different sensors which would interact with my visuals and the audio in real-time.

We showcased the project at the Societe des arts technologiques inside the Satosphere; a massive 360 degree dome.
