Human Nature

TouchDesigner, 2022

Human nature was a collaborative project with a music technology student who approached me to create the visuals for an immersive audio-visual experience that would be showcased at the Société des arts technologiques inside the Satosphere; a massive 360 degree dome.

The visuals I created were to be controlled by T-sticks, an interactive instrument that was covered in different sensors which would interact with my visuals and the audio in real-time.
