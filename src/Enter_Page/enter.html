Enter.

TouchDesigner, Kinect, 2023

Enter is an interactive art installation that was created for an event called "Interface", a one night gallery exhibition for emerging artists that centered on the theme of our relationship with technology.

My idea behind the piece was to create a visualisation of how technology surrounds us and engulfs us in the modern world.

As the user approaches images projected on the screen, a Kinect sensor tracks their movement. As they move closer, the pixels become brighter and begin to surround the user, immersing them into a sea of glowing cubes, reflecting the seductive nature of technology and our relationship with it.
